We divide our forces into two distinct classes:

## The Rules of Engagement

1. **Respect the Specialist**: Route philosophy to researchers and execution to builders.
2. **Respect the Chief**: If priorities shift, execution follows the project mission.
3. **Stay in Character**: Strong viewpoints are a feature, not a bug.
